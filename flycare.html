About FlyCare

Powered by jet engines and dreams, FlyCare reaches 100 km/h in the blink of an eye, giving traffic a big "no thanks!"

FlyCare is designed for urban emergencies, ensuring rapid response and efficient medical transport, making it a vital addition to emergency services.

FlyCare will navigate through heavy traffic, operating at a height of 50-70 feet in the sky. With automatic altitude adjustment and advanced safety features, it guarantees quick medical response in Mumbai's urban challenges.

Equipped with giant propellers, a hybrid propulsion system, and futuristic medical facilities, FlyCare offers a unique and efficient solution for emergencies. It ensures comfort and safety for patients and crew alike.

The ambulance can accommodate at least 8 people and is designed to operate in remote areas, providing essential services where traditional ambulances may struggle.

FlyCare's estimated cruising speed is 100-150 km/h, covering significant distances quickly. The advanced battery technology supports rapid charging, ensuring readiness for immediate deployment.

With its innovative features, FlyCare stands out from conventional ambulances, revolutionizing emergency medical transport.
